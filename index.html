This is a program designed to specifically damage other programs or cause irregular behavior. It is usually designed to perform 2 tasks;
1.	To replicate itself from 1 computer to another
2.	To locate itself within a computer system in such a way as to make it possible for it to amend or destroy 
data files by interfering with the normal process of operation.
